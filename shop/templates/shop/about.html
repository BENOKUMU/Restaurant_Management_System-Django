    About Us

    Welcome to The Chill Spot Food Restaurant

    Indulge in our exquisite cuisine, expertly crafted by Chef Benjamin Okumu and his talented team. Our menus feature contemporary, ingredient-focused dishes that pay homage to classic cuisine. Each dish is carefully prepared to highlight bold, vibrant flavors with a fresh, local twist. Our extensive wine list has been thoughtfully curated to offer the best in value and quality, with a wide range of varietals from around the world. Whether you're joining us for lunch or dinner, our cocktail bar and happy hour are not to be missed. And for a truly unforgettable experience, be sure to reserve our private dining room, which offers stunning views of the nearby art gallery. At Chill Spot, we pride ourselves on delivering a range of experiences to suit any mood or occasion.
